Agile Methodology

Agile software development is an approach to software development under which requirements and solutions evolve through the collaborative effort of self-organizing and cross-functional teams and their customer/end user.
Scrum Master:
The Scrum Master is responsible for maintaining the Scrum process and the overall health of the team. The Scrum Master assures that the team is fully functional and productive. A good Scrum Master proactively anticipates problems, opportunities for improvement, and conducts pre-planning so the team can focus on delivering its sprint commitments.
Scrum:
Scrum is agile methodology for project management. This methodology can be applied to nearly any project. Scrum is a daily team meeting in which every team member are bring together. It monitors progress against the feature being develop and review on a real time basis what was done yesterday. Here it is planned that what need to be done today and make sure that there is no any problem with development process.
